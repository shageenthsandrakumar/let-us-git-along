GTM Operating Archetypes

Four founder operating
modes we've identified

Your archetype determines your default GTM motion, hiring priorities, and optimal tool stack.

V

The Velocity Pair

Ship fast, measure fast, pivot fast. Optimizes for learning speed over polish. Best for: consumer apps, marketplaces.

C

The Craft Pair

Deep technical differentiation, deliberate GTM. Optimizes for product moat over speed. Best for: dev tools, infrastructure.

S

The Scale Pair

One builds the machine, one sells the vision. Optimizes for revenue velocity. Best for: B2B SaaS, enterprise.

R

The Research Pair

Long-horizon bets, deep domain expertise. Optimizes for breakthrough over iteration. Best for: biotech, AI/ML, deeptech.
