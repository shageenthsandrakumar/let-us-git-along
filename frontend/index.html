Six dimensions
What we actually measure

Most co-founder conflicts aren't about vision — they're about how people work. We measure the operational signals that predict failure.

⚡

Decision Velocity

How fast each founder moves from problem to decision. Ship-fast vs deliberate — mismatches cause constant friction.

🎲

Risk Tolerance

Comfort with ambiguity, technical debt, and unknown outcomes. Misalignment here kills momentum.

💬

Communication Style

Async vs sync preference, verbosity, and medium. Daily calls vs weekly docs — both valid, but incompatible.

🏃

Execution Pace

Sprint intensity, work cadence, sustainable pace vs burst mode. Burnout happens when paces mismatch.

🔭

Vision Alignment

Shared long-term goals, exit horizon, and definition of success. Divergence here is a ticking clock.

🤝

Conflict Resolution

Avoidant, competing, compromising, or collaborating — your style determines whether disagreements build or destroy.
