Layer 3 — Communication
The final voice
One agent reads everything every other agent produced and speaks directly to the founders as human beings.
💬

Communicator Agent

Narrative Voice

The Communicator receives the full output of every agent in the pipeline and translates all of it into flowing prose addressed directly to the founders. No bullet points, no headers, no clinical summaries. It writes like someone who has genuinely studied these two people and has something real to say to them — warm, honest, and specific enough that they think "how did it know that."

Writes

Partnership narrativeStrength synthesisFriction forecastMonth-3 agreementsClosing truth
You two are not the same kind of builder, and that is exactly the point. One of you moves on instinct and ships before the picture is complete. The other builds systems that outlast the sprint. That tension is not a warning sign — it is the engine. The question is whether you have figured out who owns the decisions that matter, and when.
GTM Archetypes

Four partnership types. One for every founding pair.

The GTM Strategist classifies every founder pair into one of four archetypes based on how they build and ship together. Each archetype comes with a tailored tool stack, operating cadence, and 30-day roadmap.

⚡

Velocity Pair

Ship fast, validate faster. Optimized for rapid iteration cycles and early traction in consumer or marketplace markets.

Fast decision + high risk
🏗

Craft Pair

Build deliberately and ship with quality. Optimized for developer tools, B2B SaaS, and markets where product depth wins.

Deliberate + systems-first
📈

Scale Pair

Metrics-led, conversion-obsessed. Optimized for growth-stage startups where channel efficiency and retention drive the outcome.

Data-driven + growth-led
🔬

Research Pair

Explore widely before committing. Optimized for deep tech, AI/ML products, and markets where the moat is built in the lab.

Experimental + insight-first
Under the hood

How it's built

FounderFit uses ag2's ConversableAgent infrastructure to run a sequential eight-agent pipeline. Each agent is a ConversableAgent with a structured system prompt that enforces JSON output. Agents run in strict order, with each agent's output passed as context to the next. The Communicator receives the full conversation history of all seven preceding agents.
